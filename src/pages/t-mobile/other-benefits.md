## What are T-Mobile’s maternity and paternity benefits?

With some restrictions, parent benefits include:

* eight weeks of paid maternity leave for the birth parent
* plus four weeks of paid family bonding time (which is also available for non-birth parents)

As a large employer, T-Mobile also must comply with the federal Family and Medical Leave Act (FMLA).

## What are T-Mobile’s dependent care benefits?

There is Free Money for Childcare (Childcare Subsidy) of:

* $250/month if annual pay is less than $63,000 or
* $175/month if annual pay is less than $99,000.

Dependent Care Flexible Spending Accounts:

* enable tax savings for dependent care expenses,
* are “use it or lose it”, and
* cannot be used for overnight camps.

## What is T-Mobile’s tuition assistance?

An employee is eligible for tuition assistance:

* after 90 days of employment, 
* if the course is directly related to a T-Mobile role, and
* is approved in advance:

T-Mobile pays schools directly for courses completed with a grade of C- or better.

T-Mobile also has relationships with multiple schools that offer a full tuition grant so the employee pays nothing out of pocket.
