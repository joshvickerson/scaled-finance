## What is T-Mobile's compensation package?

T-Mobile Pays:

* base salary and wages,
* variable bonuses or commissions,
* stock compensation, and
* many other benefits.

The mix and amount depends on level or grade.

## What is [T-Mobile's PTO policy](/t-mobile/paid-time-off)?

For full-time employees, Paid Time Off generally accrues at:

* 152 hours per year to start
* 192 hours per year after the employee’s third anniversary
* 232 hours per year after the employee’s seventh anniversary

For part-time employees, Paid Time Off generally accrues at:

* 88 hours per year to start
* 108 hours per year after the employee’s third anniversary
* 128 hours per year after the employee’s seventh anniversary

## What is T-Mobile's equity policy?

T-Mobile primarily offers the following [stock comp](/t-mobile/stock-comp):

* Restricted Stock Units (RSUs) and
* Employee Stock Purchase Plan (ESPP).

Restricted Stock Units (RSUs) vest over the course of two to three years.

The Employee Stock Purchase Plan (ESPP) offers a 15% off the lower of:

* the price at the beginning and
* end of each six month period.

{% endtextBlockWithImage %}

## What is T-Mobile's 401(k) match?

[T-Mobile matches employee 401(k) contributions](/t-mobile/401k):

* 100% on the first 3%
* 50% on the next 2%

If an employee contributes 5% of their pay, T-Mobile matches 4%.

## What are the health insurance options for T-Mobile employees?

[T-Mobile’s medical plans](/t-mobile/insurance) include:

* Copay Only Plan
* Health Savings Account (HSA)
* Health Reimbursement Account (HRA)
* Exclusive Provider Organization (EPO)

Other health-related benefits include:

* Flexible Saving Account (FSA)
* Dental plans
* Vision Service Plan (VSP)
* Life and Accidental Death & Dismemberment (AD&D)
* Short-term and long-term disability
