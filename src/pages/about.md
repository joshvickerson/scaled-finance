## Kevin Estes, CFP®, CCFC, MBA

{% textBlockWithImage "about-kevin", "/images/etes-family-picture.webp", "Image of three people standing, smiling. Kevin Estes on the right wearing a dark blue suit. Wife on the left wearing a dark green dress. Younger daughter in between wearing a dark blue dress. Light blue background." %}

I’m an hourly financial planner located near Seattle, Washington.

Before starting Scaled Finance, I worked at Amazon and T-Mobile. My experience included nine years in T-Mobile’s Financial Planning & Analysis department.

Coworkers kept asking me financial questions. I couldn’t find any financial planning firms dedicated to helping them so I decided to:

* Complete a personal financial planning program,
* Pass the CERTIFIED FINANCIAL PLANNER™ exam, and
* Found Scaled Finance, LLC.
