## Avoid wasted time and costly mistakes. Take control of your money and **live your best life**.

![5 circles: 1 magenta, SCHEDULE; 2 teal, MEET; 3 blue, DEVELOP; 4 teal, CO-CREATE; 5 magenta, IMPLEMENT. On the left: magenta You'll, teal We'll, and blue I'll vertically with the circles. 4 has magenta magenta callout: What you pay for in cursive.](/images/illustrations/our-process.webp)

{% grid id="process-steps" %}

2. Mutually determine fit, scope, and priorities.
3. Develop a roadmap based on your loosely defined goals.
4. Co-create your plan over a series of meetings.
5. Implement the changes to optimize your life.
