So Grateful 🦃

I have even more than usual to be thankful for this year:

  • Family
  • Health
  • Time
  • Clients
  • Support

Family 👨‍👩‍👧

My immediate family is incredible. Turns out, launching a business is a lot of work. 🤣 Thank you to my wife and daughter for your unwavering support!

Health 🏃‍♀️

Fortunately, we have our health! COVID-19 is starting to fade into history.

A health scare and full recovery in our extended family was top of mind this year. I'm especially grateful for the support of my sister-in-law, brother, and uncle for all their help!

Time ⏱

I'm so thankful for the flexibility my career change has given me. I've been able to take my daughter to multiple practices most weeks and attend her swim meets, band concerts, and other activities.

We also had the opportunity to go sailing for a week together in the San Juan Islands and earn our American Sailing Association certifications! My wife, daughter, and I each completed the Seafair Triathlon. 🏊‍♂️ 🚴‍♂️ 🏃‍♂️

Clients 🤝

I've been blessed to work with many clients since July. Our meetings and emails are almost always the highlights of my day. I'm inspired by the steps they're taking to build their futures!

Support 🏋‍♂️

I know I would miss thanking many wonderful people who've supported my family, me, and my business throughout the year.

Advisors and other amazingly supportive people have made this a year to remember! Thank you!
