PTO ASAP

Now is a wonderful time to book your Paid Time Off (PTO) through the rest of the calendar year!

  • Vacation rentals begin to book.
  • Flight prices start to rise.
  • Calendars get full.

Call Dibs

It’s not just the calendars of your friends and family members. You also have to worry about your coworkers’!

Holiday coverage can be a struggle for many teams. People who schedule late may have limited say in which days they can take off. 😯

Claim the days which work best for you and your family now!

Use It Or Lose It

Many companies - T-Mobile included - limit how many hours can be rolled over. Hours above the limit are forfeited / lost / involuntarily donated to the company.

Full time employees can usually roll over two weeks (80 hours) at the end of the year.

However, during my tenure with T-Mobile, it:

  • shrank to as little as 1 week (40 hours) and
  • grew to as much as 3 weeks (120 hours).

Avoid the drama. 🎭 Schedule your Paid Time Off (PTO) through the rest of the year now!
