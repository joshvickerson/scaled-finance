3 T’s

The 3 T’s of managing personal finances are:

  • Time,
  • Training, and
  • Temperament.

Goal

The intent of this video is to create space for honest self-assessment.

What you’ve done in the past may be the best predictor of what you’ll do in the future.

Time: ⏱

  • How often do you or your spouse move money around to pay bills?
  • Do your finances run smoothly, or are things sometimes missed?
  • How much time do you spend reviewing your finances, checking account balances, and evaluating investments?

Training:🎓

  • How often are you confused or surprised by your finances?
  • What proactive steps are you taking to minimize your lifetime taxes?
  • Is your money working as hard as you, or do you sense it could do more - you just don’t know what?

Temperament:😎

  • Does thinking about money keep you up at night?
  • How confident are you that your goals will be funded?
  • Did you make any investment changes during the Great Recessions of 2008 to 2009?
  • If so, what?
  • What about at the beginning of the COVID-19 pandemic?
