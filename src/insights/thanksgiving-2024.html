So Grateful 🦃

Gratitude is both free and priceless. ❤️

Thanksgiving is one of my favorite holidays. It gives me a chance to reflect on the many wonderful parts of my life, including:

  • Family
  • Health
  • Time
  • Clients
  • Support
  • Generator

Family 👨‍👩‍👧

My family is incredible! It’s been a busy year.

We’re also savoring the time we have left with our teen at home.

Health 🏃‍♀️

Fortunately, we have our health! Although we had a bout earlier this year, COVID-19 continues to fade into history.

The major health scare has passed for now. All family members who’d like to work are able!

Time ⏱

I'm so thankful for the flexibility my new career provides. I've been able to take my daughter to multiple practices most weeks and attend her various activities.

We also had the opportunity to take most of a month off this summer. We visited Yosemite National Park, San Francisco, San Juan Island, Leavenworth, and more!

Clients 🤝

I've been blessed to work with many clients. It’s hard to believe it’s only been a year and a half! They feel like family.

The highlight has been letting multiple clients know they’ve achieved financial independence!

Support 🏋‍♂️

Thank you to the many wonderful people who've supported my family, me, and my business throughout the year!

Generator ⚡

A big storm hit when we were moving to our new home a few years ago. Both homes were without power for over four days, which convinced us to buy a generator.

Thank goodness! We lost power at our house for five days starting Tuesday of last week. I'm so thankful we have, maintain, and keep fuel for our backup generator!
